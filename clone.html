ABOUT EDUFAIR

Educational fairs provide prospective students with the opportunity to meet University representatives. A fair is an event where many applicants gather and the primary topic of discussion is education either in Undergrad or for Masters. Attending fairs give you an advantage and edge. The reason many of the applicants attend the educational fair is to meet university representatives. The opportunity to meet university face to face, along with interacting with official representatives, and alumni to the highlighted advantage.

The educational consultations that areorganizing the event also provide scholarships to students that attend the fair and further apply to the university they meet.Even though yu might have a clear plan of which universities and programs you plan to apply, there is no harm in discovering options that you haven't previously considered. The course may perfectly fit your requirements and goals. Talking to as many university representatives as possible, and keeping an open mind will help you branch out if needed. Usually, many students applying to a University they met during the fair and previously had no idea about it.
